Headings and Paragraphs

Headings are used to give structure to your content. There are six levels of headings, with h1 being the most important and h6 being the least important.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs are used to group together related sentences or pieces of information. They are a great way to break up your content and make it easier to read.

Images and Videos

Images

Images are a great way to add visual interest to your webpage. You can use the <img> tag to insert an image into your page.

Videos

Videos are a great way to add dynamic content to your webpage. You can use the <video> tag to insert a video into your page.

Links

Links are a great way to connect your webpage to other pages or websites. You can use the <a> tag to create a link.

CSS

CSS is used to style your webpage. You can use CSS to change the font, color, size, and layout of your content.

JavaScript

JavaScript is used to add interactivity to your webpage. You can use JavaScript to create animations, respond to user input, and more.
